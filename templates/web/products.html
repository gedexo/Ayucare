About our Product

Ayucare  is the treatment method introduced to cure ailments through Ayurvedic methods by adding the elements of Arka Chikitsa. The treatment uses the natural herbs to make the medicines and as a result this treatment is free from side effects. Ayurvedic medicines are known as Medicine with Side benefits. The medicine is the most potent form of Ayurvedic medicine and is prepared through the process of distillation. The extracted product after distillation is called arka.

The medicine is safe for diabetics patients to consume. We provide proper consultation and treatment from qualified and dedicated doctors. The Kashaya and Massage, two major factors of traditional ayurvedic treatment, are not practiced in AYUCARE. We always keep up our excellence in caring for curing the patient.

Ayucare  is binded with ayurveda and like its ancient tradition natural herbs are used for medicinal purposes. The medicine is manufactured with keen observation and vigilance through distillation. The natural herbs in the exact proportion are distilled and extracted to get the “Arka” . Arka medicines are the most potent form of Ayurvedic medicine and are more palatable and devoid of colour and smell.

Self home therapy or DIY (Do It Yourself) products are also recommended as a part of the treatment. The medicine from natural herbs is very easy and safe to consume. Ayurvedic medicine is considered as the medicine with side benefits because unlike other medicines these medicines are free of side effects.
