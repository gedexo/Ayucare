ARKA CHIKITSA

Arka is the highly potent form of ayurvedic medicine in which the real active ingredient of the medicine is collected by the process of distillation. Arkaprakasha is the first ayurvedic authentic text about Arka Kalpana introduced in the later part of Ayurveda (AD-18) after the Samhitas period seemed to be the contribution of the great king Ravana. Later the text like Gadanigraha, Asava-Arista Vigyana and Sahasrayoga was written that describes the Arka yantra and Arka preparation. Arkaprakash by Ravan is considered a compressive referral book even though there were several books written about Arka Kalpana. Arkakalpanahas was given specific importance and it opines that it has more potency in comparison to the other kalpanas since it has all volatile active substances in the effective form in its final product. Arkakalpana is a very unique formulation in Ayurvedic Pharmaceutics for its method of preparation and efficacy.
